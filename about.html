About AETE

Empowering Future Leaders in Electronics

Founded in 1985, the Association of Electronics Engineers (AETE) at HBTU Kanpur has been at the forefront of nurturing technical excellence and innovation in electronics engineering. Our journey spans over three decades of fostering talent, promoting research, and building industry connections.
